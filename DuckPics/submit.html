How to submit photos:

  • Click 'Upload photo' to upload your photo(s).
  • Click 'Submit' for the submission of your photo(s).

Regulations:

  • Photos should be related to the Stevens Institute of Technonlogy community, campus, events, students, affiliates, etc.
  • Photos should be suitable for all ages.
    • Nudity and/or sexually-explicit imagery is prohibited.
  • Photos should be original.
  • Photos should not be mean-spirited in any way.
