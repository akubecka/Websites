Welcome To DuckPics!

DuckPics.com is a website designed by Stevens Institute of Technology students where students can post photos of the Stevens campus, its events, clubs, students, organizations, affiliates, etc. To submit a photo, head over to the submit page on our main menu, or view photos in the the gallery by going to the gallery page. If you have any questions about our site, go to the FAQ page or the contact page for more information.
